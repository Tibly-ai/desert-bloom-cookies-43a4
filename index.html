What Makes Our Cookies Different

We started with one question: why should treats taste like an ingredient list you can't pronounce? So we ditched that idea entirely.

Rolled oats, almond flour, dates, and real fruit — nothing you can't find in your own pantry.

🍯

Naturally Sweetened

Sweetened with desert honey and dates instead of refined sugar. Sweet, not sugar-crashy.
