Come Say Hi Under the Joshua Trees

Every Saturday morning we roll our little folding table into the heart of the Joshua Tree Farmers Market, set out our cookie jars, and wait for the desert wind to carry the smell of oats and honey down the aisle. Come find us — we'll have a warm sample waiting.

Parking & Getting There

Free parking is available along the market lot and on the side streets nearby. We're an easy stroll from the desert-plant nursery stand — follow the smell of fresh cinnamon.

Planning an Event? Order Ahead.

Baby showers, trail races, desert weddings — we love baking custom batches. Tell us what you need and we'll follow up with sizing and pickup details before market day.

We typically reply within two days. For same-week requests, calling ahead is best.

A Few Things Folks Ask

Do you sell anywhere besides the market?

Right now, Saturdays at Joshua Tree Farmers Market are the only regular spot — but custom orders for local pickup are always welcome.

Do you take cash and card at the stand?

Yes — we accept cash, tap-to-pay, and the usual mobile wallets. No minimum purchase required.

How far ahead should I order for an event?

One to two weeks gives us plenty of time to bake fresh, especially for larger batches or gluten-free requests.

What if it's raining on Saturday?

Desert rain is rare, but if the market closes for weather, give us a call — we may still have cookies ready for pickup.
